Account Safety

This page summarizes what you should do if your account is vulnerable

We understand that your security matters the most to you.

If you landed up here, you might have been receiving SMS and/or Emails of activities that you did not do.

Below summarizes the steps you should take to ensure your account is secured

  1. Log in to your account and view your dashboard information. Check that no information has been changed
    1. If you find any changes, change them back immediately.
    2. It is highly recommended that you change your password.

  2. While Logged in, check the Blog Post section found in the naviagtion bar. Check to see if there are any posts under your username.
    1. If there are blogs under your username that you did not write, DELETE the posts immediately.
    2. It is highly recommended that you change your password.

  3. Check other services such as Youtube, Google, etc...
    1. If your password is compromised, it is highly likely that attackers would be trying to access other services using your credentials
    2. It is highly recommended that you change your password for all sites.
